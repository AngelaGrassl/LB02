ABOUT THE BAND

Stray Kids, abbreviated to SKZ, (Korean 스트레이 키즈) is a South Korean boy group under JYP Entertainment,which was formed in 2017 as part of the reality show of the same name.

The group consists of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin and I.N. Originally a nine-piece group, member Woojin left Stray Kids due to undisclosed personal reasons in October 2019.

Stray Kids released the pre-debut EP Mixtape in January 2018, and officially debuted on March 26th with I Am Not. They released their second album I am Who on August 5, 2018 and their third album I am You on October 22, 2018.

On august 1, 2018, 5 days ahead of theri comeback, a video was uploaded on Stray Kids' YouTube channel. The video revealed the group's fandom name to be "STAY". "STAY" is "Stray" without the "r". The "r" stands for reason, meaning that their fans are their reason and where they stay.
