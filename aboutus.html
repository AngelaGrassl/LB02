ABOUT US

This website is only a fanpage and NOT the official website of Stray Kids.


The main purpose of our website is to introduce you to Stray Kids.

New STAYs can get some basic information about the group, the members, two of their newest songs and even look trough some old group photos. Of course everyone is welcome on our website, as we want to convince as many people as possible to join our fandom. We intend to make the band even more famous by drawing as much attention as possible to them.

With the release of the ODDINARY album, we wanted our website to be up to date. So we went over our design and adapted it to the concept of ODDINARY, to give you an accurate insight of the band.
